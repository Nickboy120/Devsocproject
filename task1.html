Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, commonly known as IIT Kharagpur or IITKGP, is a prestigious public technical and research university located in Kharagpur, West Bengal, India. Established in 1951, it is the oldest and largest IIT in terms of infrastructure and the number of students. The institute offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields of engineering, science, architecture, and management.
